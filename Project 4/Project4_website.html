Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views from its summit.

Opening Hours: 9 AM - 12 AM

Ticket Price: €25

Louvre Museum

The world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris.

Opening Hours: 9 AM - 6 PM

Ticket Price: €17

Notre Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, located on the Île de la Cité.

Opening Hours: 7:45 AM - 6:45 PM

Ticket Price: Free (entry to the tower is €10)

Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Perched atop Montmartre, this basilica offers stunning views of the city.

Opening Hours: 6 AM - 10:30 PM

Ticket Price: Free (entry to the dome is €6)

Local Tips

Food

Don't miss trying a traditional croissant at a local bakery!

Transportation

The metro is the fastest way to get around the city. Consider getting a Paris Visite pass for unlimited travel for a set number of days.

Culture

Be sure to greet locals with a friendly "Bonjour!" and try to learn a few basic French phrases.

Safety

Keep an eye on your belongings, especially in crowded areas, to avoid pickpockets.
